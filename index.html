ACORN

ACORN was developed by Consolidated Analysis Center, Incorporated (CACI) in London as:A Classification Of Residential Neighbourhoods and is used as a market segmentation tool, splitting the UK population into six main categories and 18 sub-groups

This has enormous applications for businesses and organisations in that use of the ACORN system provides massive amounts of data very quickly, putting it into relatable, usable information

The difference between Data and Information is that Data is raw facts and figures, whereas information is the application of such facts and figures to a given situation

E.g. The volume of shoes sold in the UK for 2014 is data, but if you were to then to pull out how many of those sold were Nike Trainers (For Example), the result would be information, because you have used the data for a particular purpose

Marketing

The CIM Definition of Marketing is:

To Attract and retain customers in a way which generates satisfaction on the part of the customer and profit on the part of the business or organisation
